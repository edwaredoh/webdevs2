What I did

My Process

After finding the template that I wanted, I began identifying which parts of the code, related to which parts of the document and how editing them would alter the document. Then I researched how different tags could be used to alter the document to my specifications.

Decisions I made

Firstly I picked my theme. I picked a theme that I felt comfortable with which was one from w3schools called simply me. The simplistic style and soft colors agreed with me.


Modifications I made

I did not make too many changes to the theme, on all pages I removed a search button. On page 3 I removed the picture and replaced it with my google map. I also changed the font of all my text to comic sans. At the top of the page I made it so that the navigation buttons would take you to other pages, instead of different parts of this page

The easy and the hard

Overall the project was fairly simple. Finding what the meanings of individual tags and how they affected the document was easy. Once I knew what they did, changing them to my liking was really simple, but the really hard part was getting the template to work in the first place.
